Take steps to Protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

COVID-19 spreads mainly from person to person through respiratory droplets. Respiratory droplets travel into the air when you cough, sneeze, talk, shout, or sing.Masks are a simple barrier to help prevent your respiratory droplets from reaching others. Studies show that masks reduce the spray of droplets when worn over the nose and mouth.

Wash Your Hands

Preventing sickness reduces the amount of antibiotics people use and the likelihood that antibiotic resistance will develop. Handwashing can prevent about 30% of diarrhea-related sicknesses and about 20% of respiratory infections Handwashing can also prevent people from getting sick with germs that are already resistant to antibiotics and that can be difficult to treat.

things not to do during covid

Public transport, metros, gyms and any other crowded place should be avoided. The more the number of people, the more likely of the spread of the virus. If you have symptoms, self-quarantine yourself other than to visit the doctor. Staying isolated and not coming in contact with a lot of people is the only way to contain the spread of the disease.

  • Do Not Share Eating
  • Do Not Touch Your Face or Nose
  • Do Not Contact Sick People
